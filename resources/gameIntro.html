Greetings,

Welcome to "Teacher vs Teacher" where minimalistic fun meets the FMI, offering a unique and straightforward gameplay experience that is sure to entertain.

How to Play

It's simple!

You only need a mouse and touchScreen for controls.

Keep your finger/mouse inside the game to not have it paused.

Touch Interaction: Touch and hold the screen for over a second to keep the game active.

Stay alert and nimble to avoid hitting any obstacles that might appear.

Quick Restart

Dying is easy. If you get hit, the game immediately restarts.

Pause and Play

The game intelligently pauses when there's no interaction, resuming only when you're ready to continue your quest.

Enemy Behaviour

Enemies spawn outside the screen, and go towards the player (mouse) faster the further they are from them.

How to Win

There isn't a specific end sequence, the goal being to get the biggest highscore through determination and patience. Score is calculated as how many tenths of a second have passed since the start of the game (excluding pauses).
